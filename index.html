I am a software engineer interested in distributed systems in general, with a focus on large-scale data storage and processing systems.

Currently, I work at Meta (formerly Facebook) on an infrastructure team that builds and operates one of the primary blob storage systems. Before Meta, I worked as a Software Engineer at WSO2 on their Identity Server product.

I completed my Ph.D. in computer science from Colorado State University in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Shrideep Pallickara. My research focused on leveraging distributed stream processing systems for voluminous data streams generated by continuous sensing environments such as IoT use-cases.

I am an open-source software enthusiast. I am a committer for the Apache Axis2 project and a two-time participant in Google Summer of Code with Apache Software Foundation.
